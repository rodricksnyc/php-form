Connect to the Presentation

To the best viewing and audio experience, connect to the event from your home desktop, laptop, or mobile device, by going to events.westat.com in your browser. Do not use your remote desktop.

Start the Webex

About 15 minutes prior to the event, or at any time during the event, the “Join Now” button will be active. Enter your name and email, and select the button to enter the event. The password will be pre-filled (the same password used to enter the website).

Play, Pause, & Stop

Once you join the event, the audio broadcast button should be active, if not hover on it to select “Play.” Once the audio is active, you can Pause or Stop the audio at any time.

Change Presentation View

For the best viewing experience, hover on the screen icon to change the view to have the presentation on the left and the speaker on the right. Full screen and zoom in and out options are available in the tool bar on the left.

Need Help?

For technical support during the event, or to access instructions, visit the links on the event landing page, events.westat.com, or send an email to pchelp@westat.com.
